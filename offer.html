Experience the charm of Maharashtra here at Bodh Valley resort. Places to see in Igatpuri are easily accessible from Bodh Valley resort. Visit Tringalwadi Lake and Tringalwadi fort in just 2 kms vicinity of our resort. Dhammagiri Fort is also easily accessible from here.
